Biography

Currently, I work as a Research Engineer at Microsoft Bing Ads team (AI+R division). I work at the selection stack of Bing Ads team and handles retrieving relevant results from a pool of millions of ads using deep semantic matching techniques. My algorithms currently generate millions of dollars on the Bing search engine.

My research interest includes natural language processing, information retrieval, deep neural networks and data analytics. I justify my role here by handling both the research aspect as well as engineering aspect by improving the search result and reducing the cost to serve respectively.
