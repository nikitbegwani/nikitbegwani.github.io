Biography

Nikit Begwani is a research engineer at the Microsoft AI+R division. He is based out of Bangalore and works with bingAds team catering for international markets. He works in the selection stack and handles retrieving relevant ads from a pool of millions of ads using deep semantic matching techniques. His research interests include computational linguistics, natural language processing, information retrieval, machine learning and deep neural networks. Currently he justifies his role by handling both the research aspect as well as engineering aspect by improving the search result and also reducing cost to serve ads.
