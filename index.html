Biography

I work as a Research Engineer II (Tech Lead) at Microsoft Bing Ads team. Deep learning models that I have developed have been deployed on millions of devices around the world and are also generating millions of dollars on the Bing search engine. My research interests are in both large scale and cross-lingual techniques for language.

I graduated from IIT Guwahati in 2017 with a major in Computer Science and a department rank of 6/88 students. My bachelor thesis was on understanding multilingual sentiment analysis on twitter data.

I am a continuous and passionate learner with an obsession to optimally solve a problem.
